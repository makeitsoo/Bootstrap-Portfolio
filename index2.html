About Me

Born in Minnesota, but mainly grew up in a small farming community in Iowa after moving there when I was 6. After graduating high school two years early, I recieved a scholarship to study automotive engineering at a local college and earned my first undergrad degree by 18. I moved to Santa Fe for a year, then to Seattle for four years, then finally landed in San Francisco where I decided to complete a B.S. in Economics at the University of San Francisco. It wasn't long after moving to Silicon Valley that I realized I wanted to be a part of the tech scene and began coding. I'm now following my passion...


I love anything having to do with the great outdoors--snowboarding, hiking, camping, biking, couple beers or a glass of wine in the park. I also love restoring old automobiles and riding motos on the beautiful winding roads across this state. But don't be fooled! I'm a real nerd at heart and can often be found hermitting out in my Cole Valley apartment. I/m a pretty serious follower of Star Trek and always enjoy a good strategy game like Civ5, and..well, pretty much anything Blizzard makes.
